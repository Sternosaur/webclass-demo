Jump, spin and smash your way into the sky as you battle Dr. Eggman with Sonic and his friends!

Sonic Jump delivers brand new, arcade style adventures. Tilt and tap your way through Story levels and
Arcade infinite modes. Visit new and classic Sonic levels for hours of fun. Challenge your friends
across the world to see who can jump the farthest.

PLAY WITH SONIC AND FRIENDS
Play as Sonic or his friends Tails, Knuckles, Amy, Rouge, Silver and Blaze. Master each characters
special abilities and attacks!

AMAZING WORLDS
Jump across the legendary Green Hill Zone or take to new worlds including Mountain, Jungle, Blue Sky
& Mechanical Zones.

STORY AND ARCADE MODES
Leap through 48 different levels on a quest to stop the evil Dr. Eggman or Jump until you drop in the
infinite Arcade Mode.

FUN CHALLENGES AND QUESTS
You gotta go fast to beat each level and win big. Every level and both game modes have special
rewards for completing special missions.

UNLOCK POWERFUL ABILITIES
Collect rings as you play the game, or purchase them in store, to unlock characters, power ups and
more.

CHALLENGE THE WORLD
Compete against players across the world to see who can jump highest in Arcade Mode or set epic
scores on global leaderboards.
